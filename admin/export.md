> Download your latest data as CSV for reporting or backups.
{.is-info}

## What you can export
- **Members**: Current membership roster.
- **Runs**: Hareline schedule with site details.
- **Run Sites**: Saved start locations and reference links.

*(screenshot: export page showing Members, Runs, Run Sites cards and Start Export button)*

## How to export
1) Open **Export Data** in the portal.
2) Choose a dataset (Members, Runs, or Run Sites).
3) Click **Start Export** to download the CSV.
