## What to include
- **Describe the issue**: What you expected vs. what happened.
- **Steps to reproduce**: List the steps and the page/run involved.
- **Context**: Browser, device, kennel, and approximate time.
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, error messages, or console logs.
- **Impact/severity**: Blocking vs. minor; how many users are affected.
