> Want to help translate GoHash into another language? Reach out and we’ll get you set up.
{.is-info}

## How to help
- Tell us which language you want to add.
- Share your GoHash account email and kennel name.
- Note any regional specifics (date/time formats, terminology).

## Contact
- Preferred: Submit a ticket at https://support.gohash.app with subject “Translations”.
- Backup: Email support@gohash.app with your language and contact details.
