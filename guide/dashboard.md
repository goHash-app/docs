> The Dashboard gives you a quick snapshot of your hareline status, member count, and alerts so you know what needs attention right now.
{.is-info}

- **Active Members**: Current active member count with a link to view the directory.
- **Hareline Status**: Weeks of runs scheduled ahead; links to view or add runs.
- **Quick Actions**: Buttons for common tasks (e.g., Add New Member, Schedule Run).
