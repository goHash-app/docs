## Members
- **Member list**: Search and filter members; view profiles and statuses.

## Add or edit a member
- **Required**: Short Name.
- **Status**: Active or Inactive.
- **Optional fields**: Full Name, Hash Name, Birthday, Join Date, Phone, Email, Nationality, Shirt size, Pants size, Notes.
- **Actions**: Save Member to create/update; Cancel to return to the list.

*(screenshot: add member form showing fields and status dropdown)*
