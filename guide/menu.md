> Manage your site navigation: add items, nest them, reorder, and point to pages or built-in views. The Home item is built-in and cannot be deleted.
{.is-info}

- **Add links**: Create items pointing to Pages, Built-in views (Home, Upcoming Runs, Past Runs), External URLs, or Top link containers.
- **Reorder**: Drag and drop to reorder items.
- **Parent/child**: Nest items under a parent; add child items to group links.
- **Built-in views**: Home, Upcoming Runs, Past Runs are provided; Home menu item cannot be removed.
- **Visibility**: Toggle an item on/off without deleting it.
