> Save and reuse your start locations so runs stay consistent and easy to schedule.
{.is-info}

- **Add a site**: Name the location and store coordinates/maps details.
- **Reuse**: Pick a saved site when creating or editing a run.
- **Edit/retire**: Update details or mark sites you no longer use.
