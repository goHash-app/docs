- **Purpose**: Explain the available roles and how to assign them.
- **Who should read**: Owners and Admins assigning permissions.

## Roles
- **Owner**: Full access, billing, domains, settings.
- **Admin**: Manage runs, members, pages, menu; billing/settings if delegated.
- **Hare Raiser**: Create/edit runs and events.
- **Hash Cash**: Billing and finance actions.
- **On Sec**: Member management and invites.
- **Scribe**: Pages and menu content.

## Best practices
- Use least privilege; assign only what’s needed.
- Members can hold multiple roles.
- Review roles periodically and after team changes.

## How to assign roles
1) Open **Administration → Users & Roles**.
2) Find the member and edit roles.
3) Save and confirm updated access.
