# Create a Kennel

- **Who can create**: Owners or admins creating the primary kennel.
- **Basic info**: Kennel name, slug/URL, and region (if applicable).
- **Contact details**: Email and primary contact for notifications.
- **Branding**: Logo/hero can be added later from Settings.

## Steps
1) From onboarding or the portal, choose **Create a Kennel**.
2) Enter name and confirm the slug/URL looks right.
3) Add contact email and save.
4) (Optional) Add branding and domains in Settings after creation.
